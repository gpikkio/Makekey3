                                                                              
                                                                              
 RadioAstron SPK Files                                                               
  =============                                                               
                                                                              
  This directory contains:

  radioastron_YYDDMM_YYDDMM.bsp       SPK orbit kernel for RadioAstron. The first YYDDMM is the start date of
                                       the orbit data, and YYDDMM is the end date.
